# 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

**Loulou Daily**
最后更新：2026年3月 / Last updated: March 2026

---

## 中文版

### 1. 概述

Loulou Daily（以下简称"本应用"）由个人开发者郭瑾开发，是一款面向海外华人的法语学习应用。本政策说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您的个人信息。

### 2. 我们收集的信息

**通过 Apple 登录时收集：**
- Apple 用户标识符（Apple ID，匿名化）
- 电子邮件地址（您可选择向本应用隐藏）
- 您在登录时填写的昵称

**使用过程中自动生成：**
- 学习打卡记录（日期）
- 应用使用行为统计（通过 PostHog，匿名化）

**存储在您本地设备上（不上传服务器）：**
- 生词本和生句本收藏内容
- 录音练习文件

### 3. 我们不收集的信息

- 精确位置信息
- 通讯录或照片
- 设备摄像头
- 麦克风录音内容（仅在设备本地处理，不上传）

### 4. 信息的使用方式

我们使用收集的信息用于：
- 提供个性化学习内容
- 保存您的学习进度和打卡记录
- 改善应用功能和用户体验（匿名统计）

我们**不会**将您的个人信息出售或共享给第三方用于商业目的。

### 5. 第三方服务

本应用使用以下第三方服务：

| 服务 | 用途 | 隐私政策 |
|------|------|----------|
| Apple Sign In | 用户登录认证 | [apple.com/privacy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/) |
| PostHog | 匿名使用统计 | [posthog.com/privacy](https://posthog.com/privacy) |
| Railway | 服务器托管（欧洲/美国节点） | [railway.app/legal/privacy](https://railway.app/legal/privacy) |
| Google Gemini API | AI 内容生成 | [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |

### 6. 数据存储与安全

- 用户账号信息存储于 Railway 云服务器的 PostgreSQL 数据库
- 数据传输全程使用 HTTPS 加密
- JWT Token 本地存储于 iOS Keychain/AppStorage，有效期 7 天

### 7. 数据保留与删除

您可以随时通过发送邮件至下方联系方式申请删除账号及所有相关数据。我们将在 30 天内完成删除。

### 8. 儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童。我们不会主动收集儿童个人信息。

### 9. 政策变更

如本政策发生重大变更，我们将通过应用内通知或更新本页面告知您。继续使用本应用即视为接受更新后的政策。

### 10. 联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请联系：
**Email：** guojin.dev@gmail.com

## English Version

### 1. Overview

Loulou Daily is developed by an individual developer (Guojin) as a French language learning app for Chinese speakers living abroad. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

### 2. Information We Collect

**Via Sign in with Apple:**
- Apple user identifier (anonymized Apple ID)
- Email address (you may choose to hide it from the app)
- Nickname entered during onboarding

**Automatically generated during use:**
- Study check-in records (dates only)
- Anonymized usage analytics (via PostHog)

**Stored locally on your device only (never uploaded):**
- Saved vocabulary and sentences (notebook)
- Voice recording practice files

### 3. Information We Do Not Collect

- Precise location data
- Contacts or photos
- Camera data
- Microphone recordings (processed locally only, never uploaded)

### 4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide personalized learning content
- Save your learning progress and check-in history
- Improve app features and user experience (anonymized analytics)

We do **not** sell or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes.

### 5. Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Apple Sign In | User authentication | [apple.com/privacy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/) |
| PostHog | Anonymous usage analytics | [posthog.com/privacy](https://posthog.com/privacy) |
| Railway | Server hosting (EU/US) | [railway.app/legal/privacy](https://railway.app/legal/privacy) |
| Google Gemini API | AI content generation | [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |

### 6. Data Storage & Security

- User account data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway
- All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS
- JWT tokens are stored in iOS AppStorage with a 7-day expiry

### 7. Data Retention & Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. We will complete the deletion within 30 days.

### 8. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

### 9. Policy Changes

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will notify you via in-app notice or by updating this page. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

### 10. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries:
**Email:** guojin.dev@gmail.com
